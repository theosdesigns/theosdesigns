Theo Martin

 

Front End Developer - HTML, CSS, Javascript

Welcome to the wonderful show of Theo Martin. I hope you enjoy what you are about to see. This is my portfolio I have been developing as a side project. Enjoy the nice space background as you scroll down.

For those who do not know this technique of moving images in websites is called parallax scrolling. Wikipedia defines parallax scrolling as a special scrolling technique in computer graphics, wherein background images move by the camera slower than foreground images, creating an illusion of depth in a 2D video game and adding to the immersion. I define it as awesome!!!

This website is made with HTML, CSS3, and to really work the magic of the parallax effect I have added a bit of jQuery. The responsive web design is done with the help of Bootstrap. I made this website just to experiment with Bootstrap and I am loving it. My navbar and stage curtain is looking flawless on tablets and mobile devices. I plan to experiment with SASS or LESS later on.

I want to build a website someday that will help kids and adults with cancer. This website will probably run on HTML, CSS, Javascript, Angular or jQuery, Ruby on Rails and SQL and of course some design stuff through Photoshop which is my favorite Adobe Product to use. I also love manipulating photos and would like to take up photography as a next hobby.
